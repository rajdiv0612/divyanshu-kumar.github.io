Project #4 – Salesforce – QuickBooks Online Integration
Sales | Finance Codleo Consulting

🛠 Salesforce · Apex · REST API · OAuth 2.0 · QuickBooks Online

  • Integrated Salesforce with QuickBooks Online to manage Customers and Invoices directly from Salesforce
  • Developed Apex REST callouts to create and update customer and invoice records in QuickBooks
  • Implemented OAuth 2.0 authentication for secure and compliant QuickBooks API connectivity
  • Stored QuickBooks Customer ID and Invoice ID in Salesforce to maintain data integrity and record mapping
  • Designed data transformation logic to map Salesforce Accounts, Opportunities, and Invoices to QuickBooks entities
  • Automated invoice creation in QuickBooks based on Salesforce record updates and business rules
  • Implemented error handling and logging mechanisms to track integration failures and ensure data consistency
  • Reduced manual accounting work by enabling real-time financial data synchronization
